About Us

Glowing Grates is Taunton's longest established heating & fireplace showroom, taking care of all your Heating, Bathroom and Stove installations. We provide comprehensive advice, high-quality professional work and an extensive level of service which is monitored at all times - both during and after the completion of the project.

And we're there for you, in person, through the whole installation. We guarantee that all of our work is carried out with the minimum of Mess, Fuss and impact on the environment. We take great pride in the loyalty of our customers, which is what motivates our continuous development as a company. The most modern and up-to-date technologies and continuous professional staff training are all priorities for us as we support you through whatever situation you find yourself in.
